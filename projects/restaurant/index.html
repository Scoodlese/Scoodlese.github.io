About Us

We are a food truck that specializes in delicious, healthy, Vegan wraps. Dont fear! We also have other options too! Our mission is to provide fresh, high-quality ingredients packed with nutrition in every meal we serve. We believe in sustainability and use eco-friendly packaging for all our meals. All our ingredients are oragniclly grown from local farms. Our menu holds a variety of wraps, salads, smoothies, other items! Come see for yourself!
